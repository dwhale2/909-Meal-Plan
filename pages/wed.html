Wednesday

Buffalo Wings

Ingredients

  • Raw chicken wings
  • Olive oil
  • Paprika
  • Ranch seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Buffalo Sauce

Instructions

  1. Thaw wings
  2. Slather wings with olive oil
  3. Season with paprika, ranch seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Put wings in air fryer
  5. Fry until cooked through. About 20 minutes.
  6. Remove from fryer, toss wings in buffalo sauce.
  7. Eat!
