The Rules

  • Each day, pick one meal and one side (side optional).
  • Each day has an option of a Devin meal, or a Caitlin meal.
  • The days are spread out so that "easy cook" and "active cook" days are alternating.
  • You may choose to eat out once a week.
  • Other meals should consist of snacks or leftovers.
  • No soda.
