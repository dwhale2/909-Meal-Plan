Thursday

Air Fryer Hamburgers

Ingredients

  • Raw hamburger patties
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Buns
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Cheese
  • Mayo

Instructions

  1. Thaw patties
  2. Season patties
  3. Place in air fryer
  4. Fry for 15 minutes, flip halfway.
  5. Put on bun with ketchup, and eat.
