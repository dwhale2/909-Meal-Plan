Monday

Beef Assistant

Ingredients

Multiply by 2 for extra.

  • Beef Assistant Box
  • 2 Cups of Milk
  • 1 Cup of Water
  • 1 lb Beef

Instructions

  1. Brown and separate the beef in a pan on HIGH.
  2. Remove beef from pan, drain grease.
  3. Add browned beef back to pan.
  4. Add 2 Cups Milk, 1 Cup Water
  5. Add flavor packet, pasta
  6. Stir together.
  7. Bring to a boil.
  8. Bring down to a simmer.
  9. Put lid on pan, cook for ~18 minutes.
