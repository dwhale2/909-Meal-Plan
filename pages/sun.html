Sunday

Fried Chicken Bites

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • Paprika
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Ranch Seasoning
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Thaw chicken breast
  2. Cut chicken into bites
  3. Create mix seasonings in bowl
  4. Massage seasonings into each bite
  5. Fry in air fryer for 15 minutes.
  6. Eat.
