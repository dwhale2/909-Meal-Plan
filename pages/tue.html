Tuesday

Oven Pizza

Ingredients

  • Frozen oven pizza
  • (Optional) Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

  1. Remove from package.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 F.
  3. Add optional cheese to pizza.
  4. Throw it into the oven.
  5. Wait 18 minutes.
  6. Pull it out. Eat it.
