Friday

Chicken/Sausage Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Smoked Sausage
  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • 3 Eggs
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Peas
  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable Oil

Instructions

  1. Fry sausage in coins with oil, set aside
  2. Chop chicken into bites, fry with oil, set aside
  3. Scramble 3 eggs on medium, chop up, set aside
  4. Add 2-3 tbsp of oil, cook peas and peppers on medium
  5. Add rice and soy sauce, stir evenly
  6. Stir in sausage, chicken, and eggs.
